Fundraising for startups

This is the place where startup founders match with global startup funding sources.

FOR ENTREPRENEURS WITH PROMISING COMPANIES

Founders, this is the place. Immediately get in front of a diverse pool of global angel investors, syndicates, VCs, and other startup investors, including the Draper Startup House Ventures Fund*, and Draper Ventures global funds.


We welcome founding teams with big ideas, at idea-stage needing smart-money support, all the way to mature-stage startups looking for international growth capital.


*Draper Startup House Ventures Fund is our own fund. We invest directly in early-stage, innovation-based startups in growth industries or in emerging technologies, at every stage.

GET THE SMARTEST SMART- MONEY ON THE PLANET

CONNECT: Find your perfect partner

Here you will find incredible people who are managing investment capital specifically targeting startups, to help you accelerate your vision beyond what you imagined.

INSPIRE: Get smart money to grow

Raise smart capital inside the Draper Venture Network, which has 24 global funds. Grow your businesses beyond the geographical borders through our worldwide network.

EMPOWER: Build the value you stand for

Ideas pivot, so investors invest in people. Surround yourself with people who strive to make you successful. Draper Startup House empowers startup founders.
